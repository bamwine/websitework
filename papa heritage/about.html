Who we are
Papa heritage is a global joint products company of Travel Tour Safaris, Print Media and real estate developers incorporated in Kampala Uganda, East Africa, great lakes region.

Papa heritage is one of the Self-adhesive and shrinkable label printing companies in the great lakes region handling Creative Designing Artworks Ripping. It was incorporated in September 2010.

With modern printing machines, Slitting, Gluing, and Cutting equipment, our qualified personnel and dedicated professionals work together as a team through innovation to develop and provide quality services including designing, manufacturing, branding and installation to meet our clients' needs.

We also provide consultancy and training on demand.
