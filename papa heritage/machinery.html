We are one of Uganda's pioneer label manufacturing companies, machine dealers and proud to be one of the top label manufacturers and machine dealers in East Africa in connection with Afriwan Company Ltd. The quality of our goods speaks for itself. Our experience stretches over several years and into every activity and field of Label making. Quality and Innovation is what separates us from the rest of the pack. Our focus is simple: to give our customers the best quality products and services.

  

The company is dedicated to manufacturing the most innovative label designers and printers in the region. You'll find these characteristics in all of our products and services, from the way we design the artwork, the careful assembly and installation of the machines, Label printing, slitting, Gluing, Cutting and packaging right down to the guaranteed long service-life of all our products that are built to exact standards.


Find out just how we do designs, printing self-adhesive, PVC sleeve shrink, BOPP labels, Metalized paper, CPP labels, Aluminum foils and branding better than the rest in Uganda.


We have well experienced creative designers with a varsity experience of 1o years of Esko file management, file ripping, logic color management, Creative designing with UNBS standards.

We do Flexo Printing, Gravure Printing, Rotogravure SYS series printing, Screen Printing, Litho-press Printing, Digital label printing.   


With modern publication and label printing equipment, our qualified personnel and dedicated professionals work together as a team through innovation, creative to develop and provide quality services including designing, manufacturing, branding and installation to meet our clients' advertising needs.

We also provide consultancy and training on demand as desired by the customers.
