COMPANY BACKGROUND

Started 2012 todate

Air Juba Company is an airline, cargo and ground handling company registered and operating in the Republic of South Sudan and was established in 2012. Its headquarters are in Juba, the Capital of South Sudan operating in all the 32 states of the country and the Abyei administrative area. Also, our partnership with DFS Middle East FZE undoubtedly provides us with an international affiliation and penetration to international markets and operations.


MISSION AND VISION

Mission :

Provision of the most reliable and efficient air travel and cargo services in the Republic of South Sudan and beyond, both local and international


Vision :

Air Juba Ltd aims to become the premiere airline operating within the Republic of SouthSudan,domestically and internationally.


CORE VALUES

Quality : Building lasting relationships with our customers by offering services that are tailored to their area of need.


Efficiency : We are dedicated to satisfying customer needs and honoring commitments that we have made to our clients at every opportunity .


Respect : we treat our team members customers, partners and suppliers with utmost and mutual respect and sensitivity, recognizing the importance of their contributions to our company’s success.


Integrity : we believe promise and trustworthiness is our most vital product. Our word is our bond. Business relations that are critical to our success depend entirely on maintaining the highest ethical and moral standards.


Air Juba Background

Our Operations

Air juba’s dedicated professionals individually accompany you every step of the way; from booking and storage of your cargo to shipment and arrival at its destination.

In the future, we plan to operate both smaller and larger capacity aircrafts to enable us service all the business requirements more efficiently. In this quest, air juba ltd intends to acquire aircrafts that are known for their efficiency and reliability, bearing in mind the infrastructural and climatic challenges posed by inadequate and sometimes poorly prepared landing strips.
